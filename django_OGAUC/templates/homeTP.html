OGAUC - Home

The observatory conducts research in astronomy and astrophysics, geophysics and geology, solar physics and spacial meteorology, space erosion, planetary sciences and the history of science.
The collected data is open for the public to see, in this website and others.
The observatory also has a museum with vast celestial and geological instruments, most from the XVIII and XIX century.
On the right is a satellite image of the observatory, located at 40.19899, -8.44277.
